\subsection{Time Series of Violent Events}

Figures~\ref{fig:ts_gaza} and~\ref{fig:ts_westbank} display the daily count of violent events
in the Gaza Strip and the West Bank, respectively, from January 2010 to May 2024.
The solid line shows the 7-day centred moving average, which smooths out day-to-day noise
and highlights medium-term trends in conflict intensity.

\begin{figure}[htbp]
    \centering
    \includegraphics[width=\linewidth]{ts_gaza}
    \caption{Daily violent events in the Gaza Strip (bars) and 7-day moving average (line),
             January 2010 -- April 2024. Violent events include battles,
             explosions/remote violence, violence against civilians, and riots.
             Source: ACLED.}
    \label{fig:ts_gaza}
\end{figure}

\begin{figure}[htbp]
